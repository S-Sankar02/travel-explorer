Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save destinations to favorites?

Simply click the heart icon on any destination card or in the destination details modal to add it to your favorites.

Is the weather information real-time?

Yes! We use OpenWeatherMap API to provide real-time weather data for all destinations.

Can I suggest new destinations?

Absolutely! We're always looking to expand our collection. Use the contact form to suggest new places.

Do you offer travel booking services?

Currently, we focus on destination discovery. However, we're working on integrating booking features in the future.
